Hello there, and welcome to my homepage!

My name is Chitra Karki, and I am a Ph.D. candidate in Data Science at the University of Texas at El Paso. My research focuses on pathogen-related proteins, their interactions, and applying computational and statistical methods to understand virulence mechanisms. I also work on developing bioinformatics tools and implementing machine learning techniques for biological data.

I maintain a list of my publications, research projects, and implementations under the Research tab, and a list of other projects under the Projects tab. You can also check out my GitHub profile for code and tools.

For a comprehensive overview of my work, you can view my curriculum vitae. I also share occasional blog posts on research and computational methods here. To learn more about my background and interests, check out the About Me page.
